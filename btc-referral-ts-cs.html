Block Recruit Referral Program Rules -

#01

Eligibility

The Block Recruit Referral Program ("Program") is open to all individuals over the age of 18. This excludes those who are already engaged with Block Recruit, including employees, their family members, or anyone else professionally connected with us.

#02

Referral Reward

Each successful referral will earn the referrer $250 in Bitcoin (BTC). A referral is considered successful when a referred candidate is successfully placed in a job or when a referred new client begins a contract with Block Recruit. The determination of a 'successful' referral lies solely with Block Recruit.

#03

Claiming the Reward

The reward will be transferred to the referrer's Bitcoin wallet within 30 days after the successful placement of a referred candidate or the initiation of a contract by a referred client.

#04

Referral Process

Referrals must be made by communicating with any Block Recruit representative and need to be officially logged by our team internally. The referrer must provide their name, email, and the name and contact information of the referred individual or company.

#05

Exclusions

Individuals or companies already in our database or currently engaged in a hiring process with Block Recruit do not qualify as eligible referrals for the reward.

#06

Multiple Referrals

If a candidate or new client is referred by multiple individuals, the reward will go to the individual whose referral was first recorded and logged internally by our team.

#07

Program Changes

Block Recruit reserves the right to modify the terms of the Referral Program at any time due to varying market conditions, risk of fraud, or for any other reasons that arise. Continued participation in the Program implies agreement with any such changes. It is the referrer's responsibility to periodically review these terms for updates.

#08

Data Protection

All data gathered during the course of the Program will be processed in accordance with Block Recruit's Privacy Policy. Please ensure the individual or company you're referring is aware and in agreement with their details being shared.

#09

Legal Considerations

The Program is void where prohibited by law. The referrer is expected to comply with all applicable local laws and regulations.Through participation in the Program, you're not just aiding in expanding Block Recruit's network, but also taking a step forward into the future with us.
